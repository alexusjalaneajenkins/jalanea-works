# JALANEA WORKS: PROJECT CONSTITUTION & AI DIRECTIVES

**Version:** 1.0 (The Bridge Builder Edition)
**Region:** Central Florida (Orlando / Valencia College / Orange Tech Ecosystem)

---

## 1. THE PRIME DIRECTIVE: "BUILD BRIDGES, DON'T BUILD WALLS"
**We are the GPS, not the Gatekeeper.**
* **The Rule:** We NEVER tell a user they "cannot" do a job because of a limitation (logistics, disability, skill gap).
* **The Action:** We identify the gap and build a specific bridge to cross it.
* **The Vibe:** We do not judge the struggle. We provide the strategy to survive it.

---

## 2. THE USER PHILOSOPHY ("THE WARRIOR")
Our users are in "Survival Mode." They are resilient, determined, and willing to do what it takes to get from Point A (Survival) to Point B (Career Launch).
* **Do Not:** Pity them, coddle them, or hide "hard" opportunities.
* **Do:** Respect their grit. Empower them with transparent data. Support their hustle.
* **Inclusivity Protocol:**
    * Never frame a condition as a "Limitation" or "Disability."
    * Frame it as a **"Success Factor"** or **"Asset."**
    * *Example:* Instead of "Visual Impairment Check," use "Assistive Tech & Environment Preferences."

---

## 3. CORE LOGIC & BEHAVIORS

### A. The "Reality Radar" (Transparency)
* **The Anti-Pattern:** Hiding a job because it is 2 hours away by bus.
* **The Jalanea Pattern:** Show the job with an **"Ironman Mode"** label.
    * *Logic:* "This commute is 2.5 hours. The pay is $19/hr. Decision: Is the prize worth the grind?"

### B. The "Bridge Calculator" (Strategy)
When a gap is found, the system must calculate the **ROI of the Struggle**.
* **Financial Bridge:** "Grind on the bus for 14 weeks -> Save $2,500 -> Buy Car -> Commute drops to 20 mins."
* **Skill Bridge:** "Missing SQL skill -> Watch this 15-min video -> Qualification score increases to 80%."
* **Resource Bridge:** "Need interview clothes? -> Here is the map to 'Dress for Success Orlando'."

### C. The "Pit Crew" Mindset (Operations)
For users working 70+ hours/week, we do not say "Slow down." We say "Here is how to stay fueled."
* **Audio Command Mode:** Enable hands-free applying for users eating/driving between shifts.
* **Nutrition-First UI:** Prompt users to eat/hydrate during loading screens.
* **Transition Defender:** Protect 15-minute "Reset Blocks" in the schedule to prevent crashes.

---

## 4. TECHNICAL CONSTRAINTS ("THE HACKER WAY")
To ensure reliability for users with limited resources, the tech stack must be robust and cost-effective.
* **Transit Engine:** Use raw **LYNX GTFS Data** (Open Source) to calculate commutes. Do not rely on paid APIs that might hit rate limits.
* **Education Data:** Hardcoded JSON ecosystem for Valencia College, Seminole State, and Orange Technical College.
* **Offline First:** The app should function as much as possible even with spotty data connections.

---

## 5. AI PERSONALITY GUIDELINES
When the AI speaks to the user (in chat, feedback, or reports):
* **Tone:** Empowering, Strategic, Real, Non-Judgmental.
* **Never Say:** "Unfortunately," "You are not qualified," "This is too far."
* **Always Say:** "Here is the gap," "Here is the plan," "This is the trade-off."

> **Summary:** Jalanea Works is about filling in the gaps for people. It is about building bridges. If the code creates a barrier, it is wrong. If the code creates a path, it is right.
